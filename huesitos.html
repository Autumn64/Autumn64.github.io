¡Alcánzalos, Huesitos!

Game made as a project for the UVM National Engineering Forum 2024. (In Spanish)

Controls:

Run: Arrows, A, D

Jump: Space

Options and pause: Return

Credit screen: Backspace

Support for Xbox, Playstation and Nintendo Switch controllers, and touch screens (EXPERIMENTAL) included.

Easter egg: MINUS1
