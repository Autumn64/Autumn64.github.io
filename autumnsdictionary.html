Furruco

Language
Spanish

Etymology
Corruption of Farruko

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /fu'ruko/
  • Syllabification: fu‧rru‧co

Noun
furruco m (plural furrucos, feminine furruca, feminine plural furrucas)

  1. (slang) weirdo
  2. (slang, a bit offensive) stupid, idiot

Usage notes
Can be used at any informal situation.

Mierdonga

Language
Spanish

Etymology
From Spanish mierda + English dong + a

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /mjeɾˈdoŋga/
  • Syllabification: mier‧don‧ga

Noun
mierdonga m or f (plural mierdongas)

  1. (slang, vulgar) shit
  2. (vulgar) shit, bullshit
    • ¡Vaya mierdonga!
      • This is bullshit!
      • (literally, "what a shit")
    • No seas mierdonga
      • Don't be a jerk
      • (literally, "don't be a shit")

Putanga

Language
Spanish

Etymology
Derived from Tagalog putang ina

Pronunciation

  • IPA(key): /puˈtaŋga/
  • Syllabification: pu‧tan‧ga

Noun
putanga m or f (plural putangas)

  1. (slang, vulgar, offensive) motherfucker
    • ¡Hay que ser putanga!
      • You got to be a motherfucker!
      • (literally, "it has to be a motherfucker")
    • Eres un tremendo putanga
      • You're such a motherfucker
  2. (vulgar) shit, bullshit
    • ¡Qué putanga!
      • What a bullshit!
    • Te pasaste de putanga
      • You went too far, you crossed the line
      • (literally, "you passed the shit")

Interjection
¡putanga!

  1. (vulgar) shit! (expression of worry, failure, shock, only for negative situations)
    • Putanga!
      • Shit!
  2. (vulgar) fuck!
    • ¡Qué putanga!
      • Fuck!
